Affogato

Affogato is a delightful Italian dessert that combines two beloved ingredients: espresso and gelato (or ice cream). The word "affogato" translates to "drowned" in Italian, referring to the way the espresso is poured over the gelato, creating a delicious mix of hot and cold, bitter and sweet.

$30

Caffè Americano

Caffè Americano is a popular coffee drink made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee but with a different flavor profile. The name "Americano" is said to have originated during World War II when American soldiers in Italy would dilute espresso to make it more similar to the coffee they were used to back home.

$20

Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are often enjoyed as a morning or mid-day treat and can be customized with flavored syrups or toppings such as chocolate powder or cinnamon. The ratio of espresso to milk can also be adjusted to suit individual preferences, with some preferring a stronger coffee flavor and others opting for a more milky taste.

Espresso

Espresso is the foundation for a variety of coffee beverages, including cappuccinos, lattes, and macchiatos. It is typically served in a small cup called a demitasse and enjoyed for its bold flavor and caffeine kick.
